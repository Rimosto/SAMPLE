About Naivasha ECDE

Our Vision

A high quality education for sustainable development.

Our Mission

To provide quality education of curriculum based education in pre primary schools.

Our Core Values

  • Quality Education
  • Child-Centered Approach
  • Professionalism
  • Integrity and Accountability
  • Community Partnership
  • Innovation and Creativity

Quality Education

Implementing competency-based curriculum with child-centered approaches for holistic development.

Health & Nutrition

Providing school feeding programs and health checks to ensure learners' wellbeing.

Community Engagement

Working with parents and local communities to create supportive learning environments.
