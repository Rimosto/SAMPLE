About Naivasha ECDE

Our Mission

To provide quality early childhood education through data-driven insights, community engagement, and sustainable development practices that nurture every child's potential.

We believe that investing in early childhood education lays the foundation for lifelong learning, well-being, and community development.

Strategic Goals

  • Increase ECDE enrollment to 95% by 2026
  • Train 100% of ECDE teachers in modern pedagogy
  • Improve infrastructure in all ECDE centers
  • Enhance parental involvement in early education

Quality Education

Implementing competency-based curriculum with child-centered approaches for holistic development.

Health & Nutrition

Providing school feeding programs and health checks to ensure children's wellbeing.

Community Engagement

Working with parents and local communities to create supportive learning environments.
